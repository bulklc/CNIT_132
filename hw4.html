Elizabeth Stewart Elementary




Elizabeth Stewart is a Dual Language Immersion Elementary School in West Contra Costa Unified School District (WCCUSD) serving students from Kindergarten through Fifth Grade. Located atop one of Pinole’s many hills, Elizabeth Stewart serves a diverse student population of about four hundred and sixty students.

Elizabeth Stewart’s dedicated Educators pursue three primary goals:
  • Create a positive learning environment around Safety, Respect and Responsibility
  • Promote digital literacy and responsibility to prepare students for tomorrow's challenges.
  • Engage and strengthen Student's creativity and willingness to engage in crtical analysis.
The School's campus is located at 2040 Hoke Dr, Pinole, CA 94564. The front office can be reached at (510) 231-1410.
